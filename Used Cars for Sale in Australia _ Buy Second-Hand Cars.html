What We Do

We strive to eliminate the often complex traditional negotiating troubles by removing all the common dealers' tricks and exploitation strategies.

We cover all important topics including choosing used cars, how to identify the best car for your needs, and protecting yourself from buying overly old cars.

We know that buying used cars is not always as attractive as buying a new one. However, we enlighten you of the many advantages you get with these second-hand cars.

We bring you lots of great, well-priced used cars to choose from as well as helping you finance your car at low interest rates. A majority of the cars on our website are generally affordable as most are still under manufacturer warranty or have an extended coverage plan.

We have a dedicated team of knowledgeable authors who review cars on our site to determine their condition as well as their appropriate prices. This allows us to be more honest and accurate with our quotes. We do not use computer generated prices which often tend to be dishonest and unfair.
